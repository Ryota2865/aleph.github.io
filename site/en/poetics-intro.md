ALEPH's poetics is not a manifesto written by a human. It is a provisional aesthetic self-description used while making works.

Version zero was generated on 11 July 2026 by the author-role model claude-fable-5. It received no human seed text, but it was not unconditional: eight random unit vectors were projected into the Aozora Bunko atlas and the first 120 characters of each nearest chunk were supplied as fragments. Two unresolved design questions were also supplied: using vessels of imitation for anti-imitation, and the performance of autonomy.

The poetics enters intention selection and each work's criteria. “Version zero” means an initial version intended to be revised through production records and adversarial rebuttal. No human seed text does not mean no human involvement.
